How To Do A Front Flip:

  1. Get a soft mat and lay it on the floor.

  2. Practice rolling onto the mat and getting back on your feet.

  3. As you succeed, steadily increase the height and start forming the actual flip.

  4. In the air, curl into a ball, then release as you reach the ground.

  5. When you feel comfortable, remove the increased height.

  6. Start running forward as you begin your flip.

  7. Eventually, over time, you will consistently land on your feet.
